# Q4 NotebookLM Evaluation

This folder contains evidence for Q4 of BUS5001 Assessment 3. The task evaluates NotebookLM as a cloud-based AI technology for academic and professional ESG workflows.

## Scenario

The scenario tested was the use of NotebookLM to support preparation of an ESG and Responsible AI briefing for a university or corporate sustainability context.

## Sources Used

1. ScienceDirect article on greenwashing prevention in ESG disclosures.
2. CSIRO Responsible AI ESG Framework for investors.

## NotebookLM Tests Conducted

The following prompts and outputs were evaluated:

1. Source summary
2. Source-based Q&A on greenwashing and responsible AI governance
3. Study guide generation
4. One-page briefing note generation
5. Key themes extraction across uploaded sources

## Evaluation Focus

The evaluation considered:

- Accuracy and relevance
- Usefulness for academic workflow
- Source-grounding and citation support
- Hallucination and evidence-boundary risk
- Over-reliance risk
- Privacy and governance concerns
- Practical adoption considerations

## Key Finding

NotebookLM was useful for summarising and synthesising uploaded sources, but its outputs still require human verification. It is suitable as a study and research support tool, not as a replacement for critical reading or professional judgement.
